KP Korea Software to Acquire Office in Pyongyang

New office accelerates development process and improves scalability.



KP Korea Software has recently been awarded a business office by Respected Comrade Kim Jong-Un for the organization's outstanding performance and loyalty to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.


Located in the heart of Pyongyang, South of the Embassy of Egypt, KP Korea Software's new headquarters is the perfect place for a growing organization. The new office acts as a scalable solution, allowing the organization to recruit more members and invest more time into software production.
