About Us

Our objectives, initiatives, and goals.

2QMM+262, Pyongyang, Democratic People's Republic of Korea



KP Korea Software is a software development and research organization based in the capital city Pyongyang of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea (DPRK). Founded on the principles of scientific independence and national self-reliance, KP Korea Software is committed to creating innovative digital technologies directed towards a global audience.


KP Korea Software is responsible for a wide range of projects, including operating systems, system management utilities, user programs, cybersecurity software, development resources, and more. We take pride in our ability to independently develop an extensive variety of portable and scalable software solutions.


We at KP Korea Software are open to select forms of international collaboration and development outsourcing, offering reliable engineering capabilities grounded in disciplined research and technical rigor. By combining structured methodology with independent innovation, we aim to position ourselves as a forward-looking contributor to the global software industry. Please see our Contact page to inquire about outsourced software development.
